Convert a sparse matrix from ST to DIA format.
ST: sparse triplet,    I, J,  A.

  The matrix in ST format:
     #     I     J       A
  ----  ----  ----  --------------

     0     1     1           1
     1     1     2           7
     2     2     2           2
     3     2     3           8
     4     3     1           5
     5     3     3           3
     6     3     4           9
     7     4     2           6
     8     4     4           4

DIA Matrix:
  0:	1.000000	2.000000	3.000000	4.000000	
  1:	7.000000	8.000000	9.000000	0.000000	
 -2:	0.000000	0.000000	5.000000	6.000000
