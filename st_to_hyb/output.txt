 Convert a sparse matrix from ST to DIA format.
 ST: sparse triplet,    I, J,  A.

  The matrix in ST format:
     #     I     J       A
  ----  ----  ----  --------------

     0     0     0           1
     1     0     1           7
     2     1     1           2
     3     1     2           8
     4     2     0           5
     5     2     2           3
     6     2     3           9
     7     3     1           6
     8     3     3           4

------------------------------
The final HYB format martrix: 

	ELL Column indices: 
	0         1         
	1         2         
	0         2         
	1         3         
	
	ELL Values: 
	1.0000    7.0000    
	2.0000    8.0000    
	5.0000    3.0000    
	6.0000    4.0000    
	
	ST Martrix: 
	i         j         v         
	------------------------------
	2         3         9.0000
