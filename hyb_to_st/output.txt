Convert a sparse matrix from HYB to ST format.

------------------------------
The original HYB format martrix: 

	ELL Column indices: 
	0         -1        
	1         2         
	0         2         
	1         3         
	
	ELL Values: 
	1.0000    0.0000    
	2.0000    8.0000    
	5.0000    3.0000    
	6.0000    4.0000    
	
	ST Martrix: 
	I         J         V         
	------------------------------
	2         3         9.0000    

ST: sparse triplet,    I, J,  A.
  The matrix in ST format:
     #     I     J       A
  ----  ----  ----  --------------

     0     0     0           1
     1     1     1           2
     2     1     2           8
     3     2     0           5
     4     2     2           3
     5     3     1           6
     6     3     3           4
     7     2     3           9
