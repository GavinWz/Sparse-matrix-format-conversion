Convert a sparse matrix from HYB to ST format.

------------------------------
The original HYB format martrix: 

	ELL Column indices: 
	0         3         
	0         1         
	0         1         
	1         3         
	0         2         
	
	ELL Values: 
	1.0000    9.0000    
	5.0000    7.0000    
	3.0000    6.0000    
	4.0000    8.0000    
	1.0000    2.0000    
	
	ST Martrix: 
	I         J         V         
	------------------------------
	1         4         2.0000    
	2         2         2.0000    
	2         4         7.0000    
	4         3         7.0000    
	4         4         5.0000    

ST: sparse triplet,    I, J,  A.
  The matrix in ST format:
     #     I     J       A
  ----  ----  ----  --------------

     0     0     0           1
     1     0     3           9
     2     1     0           5
     3     1     1           7
     4     2     0           3
     5     2     1           6
     6     3     1           4
     7     3     3           8
     8     4     0           1
     9     4     2           2
    10     1     4           2
    11     2     2           2
    12     2     4           7
    13     4     3           7
    14     4     4           5
